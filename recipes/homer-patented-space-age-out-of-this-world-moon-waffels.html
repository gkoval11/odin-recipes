Homer's Patented Space-Age Out-of-This-World Moon Waffles

Description:

Home alone while your family is stuck at church? Wells it's the perfect time to make Homer's patented space-age out-of-this-world moon waffles. The perfect combination of sweet, smokey, and fattening, this recipe will make sure you start your day right.

As seen on: Homer the Heretic (Season 4 Episode 3)

Ingredients:

  • Soft carmels
  • Premade waffle batter (either make from a mix or homemade waffle batter)
  • Liquid smoke
  • 1 stick of butter

Steps:

  1. Plug in your waffle iron till hot.
  2. Directly onto the waffle iron, add a layer of your carmels, then a layer of waffle batter, and finally as much liquid smoke as desired.
  3. Close the lid of the waffle iron and cook following the manufacturer's instruction (usually about 5 minutes).
  4. There should be a good amount of overflow: the waffle runoff. Scoop up some of the runoff with your finger and eat the batter. Caution: do not touch the hot waffle iron.
  5. When the waffle is cooked, open the waffle iron lid and peel it off.
  6. Wrap the waffle around a whole stick of butter and skewer with a toothpick.
  7. Keep repeating till you run out of carmels or waffle batter.

P.S. don't forget to clean the waffle iron immediately after you finish with the waffles. Letting the carmel cool too much will make it much harder to clean the waffle iron.
