Chocolate Chocolate Chip Cookies

Image source: Sally's Baking Recipes

Description:

What's an easy way to improve upon a chocolate chip cookies? Add more chocolate! This recipe is for a chocolate chocolate chip cookies (also known as a double chocolate chip cookies). The cookie dough itself is chocolate flavored with the addition of cocoa powder and stil has chocolate chips. This recipe is borrowed from allrecipes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 cup of unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup of cocoa powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degreed F (175 degrees C).
  2. Beat the sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla in a large bowl until light and fluffy.
  3. Combine the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in another bowl; stir into the butter mixture until well blended. Mix in the chocolate chips. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto an ungreased cookie sheets.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until just set, 8 to 10 minutes. Cool slightly on the cookie sheet before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
